BIO

Hailing proudly from the Mother City of Cape Town and purveyor of infectious dance grooves, Conduit's music selection embraces a solid electronic aggression. This translates into sets where he picks apart & infuses together PsyTech music that tantalizes your senses.

His unique fusion of Galactic Progressive, Techno & PsyTrance delivers sophisticated beats with cosmic psy melodies and sneaky drops of driving techno bass lines.

"For me, it's all about the Music. It inspires and provokes us to be who we are to the fullest of our abilities. I imagine that music plays many roles in your life as it does in mine. It’s an element that connects us, allowing others to have a sense of who we are and what we believe. Consider the ways that music has influenced your life. Music has the power to shift our feelings from somber to euphoria in an instant. It can bring us back to memories we forgot we had, or forward to the world we have yet to create. It's an incredible high playing music for a crowd...tapping into that shared energy and emotion...taking them on a Journey! I'm just a Conduit"
